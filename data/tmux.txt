\033[1mcolcon build\033[0m - Terminal Multiplexer Quickreference\n
SESSIONS\n
tmux ls \n\tList currently running sessions.\n
tmux new -s <session_name> \n\tCreate a new session with a specified name.\n
tmux a -t <session_name> \n\tAttach to running session.\n
tmux d \n\tDetach from current session.\n
tmux kill-ses -t <session_name> \n\tKill session.
